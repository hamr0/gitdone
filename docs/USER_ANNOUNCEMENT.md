# New Feature: Platform Statistics Dashboard

**Exciting News!**

We're launching a new **Platform Statistics Dashboard** on the GitDone landing page to help you track event activity at a glance.

---

## What You'll See

When you visit the GitDone landing page, you'll notice a new "Platform Statistics" section at the bottom showing:

- **Total Events** — How many events exist on the platform
- **Total Steps** — Total workflow steps across all events
- **Completed Events** — Events where all steps are finished
- **Completed Steps** — Workflow steps that are complete

The statistics update automatically every 6 hours, so you always see recent data.

---

## Why This Matters

✓ **Quick Overview** — See platform activity without digging through reports
✓ **Track Progress** — Monitor how many events are complete
✓ **Plan Better** — Understand platform capacity and trends
✓ **Automatic Updates** — No manual work needed; updates happen in the background

---

## How to Use It

1. Visit the GitDone landing page
2. Scroll to the bottom
3. Look for "Platform Statistics"
4. View the metrics in the table

**That's it!** No new buttons to click, no new workflows to learn.

---

## Will This Affect My Work?

**No.** This feature is read-only and displays existing data. Your event creation, vendor management, and workflow processes remain exactly the same.

---

## Questions?

If you have questions or notice anything unexpected:
- Check that you're viewing the latest version of the site (refresh your browser)
- Contact the support team if the statistics seem incorrect or unavailable
